# HR Analytics - Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to identify the key factors contributing to employee turnover and provide actionable insights to improve employee retention.

## Business Problem

Employee attrition can lead to increased recruitment costs, reduced productivity, and loss of organizational knowledge. This project helps HR teams understand the reasons behind employee departures and supports data-driven decision-making.

## Key KPIs

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate
* Average Monthly Income
* Department-wise Attrition
* Job Role-wise Attrition

* Job Satisfaction Analysis

## Key Findings

* Overtime employees have higher attrition.
* Lower salary groups show higher turnover.
* Employees with poor work-life balance are more likely to leave.
* Limited promotion opportunities increase attrition risk.
* Younger employees demonstrate higher attrition rates.

## Business Recommendations

* Reduce excessive overtime.
* Improve employee engagement programs.
* Review compensation policies.
* Strengthen career growth opportunities.
* Enhance work-life balance initiatives.
